🧭 Built a free travel risk intelligence tool because I was tired of scattered government advisories.

Aggregates risk data from Spain (MAEC), US State Dept, and UK FCDO. Cross-references them so you can see discrepancies at a glance.

Also includes:
• Real-time incident monitoring
• Embassy/consulate contacts
• Visa requirements
• Emergency mode with local numbers
• Flight delay checker + EU261 claim generator

No account needed for basics. Made it for myself, keeping it open for everyone.
